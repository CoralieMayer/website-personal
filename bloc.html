Bloc Finance

Undocumented immigrants typically face daunting challenges when trying to use the banking system in their new country. Bloc is a blockchain-powered digital wallet that enables them to budget, save and make payments from any location without having to use a bank. It also gives them direct access to humanitarian financial aid and the ability to make peer-to-peer transactions.

Winner of the RGD Gravity Inc. Award for Social Good 2021.
