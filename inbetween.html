inBetween

Second generation immigrant youth and young adults face a unique set of challenges around their identity. InBetween is a social connection app designed to make exploring second gen identity more accessible. The app uses shared experiences to bring together a welcoming digital community and to create a sense of belonging.

Thank-you to my research partners Rocio Palomar Robisco, Emma Sato and Marina Subach.
